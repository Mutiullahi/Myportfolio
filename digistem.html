Digistem is an Affilate marketing platform

  • What is Digistem all about

Digitstem is an all-in-one online marketplace designed to cater to the needs of vendors, affiliates, and customers. Our platform offers a unique opportunity for affiliates to earn commissions by recommending products to customers. A vendor refers to an individual who owns a product that is available for sale on Digitstem. Affiliates, on the other hand, are marketers who promote these products to potential customers. Whenever a referred customer makes a purchase, the affiliate earns a commission. Our platform is user-friendly and easy to navigate. To get started, simply use the unique link provided by our system for each product. Our highly effective tracking system ensures that affiliates are fairly compensated for the sales they refer. At Digitstem, we prioritize transparency and fairness in all our dealings with vendors and affiliates. Finally, customers can enjoy a seamless shopping experience with access to a wide range of high-quality products available on our platform. We strive to provide our customers with exceptional service and reliable products that meet their needs.

How is commission shared between vendors and affilates ?

At Digitstem, we recognize the importance of incentivizing affiliates to promote vendors' products. While vendors have the ultimate say in the commission rates offered to affiliates, we strongly advise them to provide reasonable commissions to ensure that affiliates are motivated to promote their products effectively. Our team works closely with vendors to help them determine the appropriate commission rates that align with market standards and fair compensation for affiliates. We believe that fair and reasonable commission rates are key to building a successful and sustainable online marketplace that benefits all parties involved. Therefore, we strive to facilitate open communication between vendors and affiliates to ensure a mutually beneficial partnership that promotes growth and success.
